How it works

Simple RP‑friendly flow. No player accounts, just receipts.

  • 1. Open the Catalogue and add craftable items to your order.
  • 2. Go to Checkout, enter your in‑game name, phone, and bank number.
  • 3. Submit to generate a unique BusterDeals receipt code.
  • 4. Show that code to Ollie Buster in‑game via text, phone, or in person.
